In this presentation, I’m sharing a selection of photos that highlight some of Berlin’s most iconic and culturally significant landmarks. These images capture the diversity and historical richness of the city, starting with the Gedächtniskirche, a striking reminder of World War II, and the Brandenburger Tor, one of Germany’s most recognized symbols.

You’ll also see the Siegessäule, Reichstag, and Bellevue Palace, each representing different eras of German history and governance. Alongside these are cultural venues like the Haus der Kulturen der Welt, Theater des Westens, and the Metropol Theater, which showcase Berlin’s vibrant artistic and architectural landscape.
